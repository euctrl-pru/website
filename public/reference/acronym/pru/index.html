PRU - Performance Review Unit

The PRU is responsible for monitoring and reviewing the performance of the European ANS System. Based on its analysis, the PRU supports the PRC in improving the performance of the European ANS system.

Organisationally, PRU is part of the EUROCONTROL Single Sky Directorate.

At system level (ESRA08), traffic increased by 3.9% in the first Quarter 2017 which corresponds to 907 additional flights per day, on average.
At the same time, average daily en-route service units (SU) grew by 5.7% in the first Quarter 2017 compared to the same period in 2016.

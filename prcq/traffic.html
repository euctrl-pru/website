At system level (ESRA08), traffic increased by 4.1% in the first six month in 2017 compared to the same period in 2016 which corresponds to 1069 additional flights per day, on average.
At the same time, average daily en-route service units (SU) grew by 6.5% in the first half of 2017 compared to the same period in 2016.

Traffic variation at the top 30 airports in terms of traffic

Jan-Jun 2017 vs. Jan-Jun 2016
Amsterdam airport was the aiport with the highest number of commercial movements in the first six months in 2017.
The two Istanbul airports and Rome Fiumicino showed the highest decrease in traffic between January and June 2017.
The highest growth was observed for Brussels airport followed by Lisbon, Manchester, and Warsaw.

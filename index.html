The Performance Review Unit (PRU) is the supporting Unit of the Performance Review Commission (PRC) and the Performance Review Body (PRB).

Organisationally, it is part of the EUROCONTROL Agencyâ€™s Pan-European Single Sky Directorate.

The PRU is responsible for monitoring and reviewing the performance of the European ANS System.
Based on its analysis, the PRU supports the PRC and the PRB for performance improvements in the European ANS system.

In performing its tasks, the PRU maintains an independent view and transparent working arrangements with service providers, airspace user organisations and industry.
